Varioskan LUX Plate Reader

Preparation

  1. Pipette your samples in an appropriate scheme on a well plate. An example scheme from the undergrad labs is shown below. Include blanks, standards, and your sample


Acquisition

  1. Start the SkanIt software on the attached PC

  2. Click Other instrument types and select New session for Varioskan LUX

  3. Select Plate Layout from the tree on the left

  4. In the Plate template dropdown menu, select the appropriate plate for analysis, most likely Corning, U-bottom, 96-well

  5. Populate the wells in the software according to their sample type. You can drag the cursor to populate multiple wells

  6. Select Protocol in the session tree and click Absorbance, Fluorescence, Luminescence, or TRF depending on your assay. Input the appropriate parameters, e.g.Â measurement wavelength for Absorbance

  7. Optional Select Protocol in the session tree to change measurement order and delay

  8. Optional Select Report to customize the output of your results

  9. In the bottom left of the window, click the button with the red arrow to eject the plate reader tray


  10. Insert the plate into the tray with the lid off. Leaving the lid on will cause a jam

  11. Click the button with the green upwards arrow to insert the plate into the instrument

  12. Click Start to begin the assay. Save your experiment with the appropriate directory and filename

Reporting

  1. Under the Report branch, click Summary. From the top right, select your desired reporting format to export.
